Required when your down payment is below 20%. It protects the lender if you default — the premium is added to your mortgage principal, not paid upfront.

GDS Affordability

Gross Debt Service ratio: mortgage P&I + property tax + heating + 50% of condo fees, as a % of gross household income. Lenders typically cap this at 39%.
